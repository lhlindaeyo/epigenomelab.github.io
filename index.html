Research Interest

At Epigenome Lab, we explore the epigenomic foundations of human health, with a focus on cancer and non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD). Through data-driven research and genomic innovation, we aim to uncover molecular mechanisms and translate them into better strategies for diagnosis, treatment, and prevention.
